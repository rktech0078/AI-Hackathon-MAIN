# Chapter 27: Midterm Simulation Project

**Objective:** Build a complete Digital Twin of a warehouse robot.

## Requirements

1.  **Robot Model:**
    *   Differential drive chassis.
    *   Lidar sensor mounted on top.
    *   Camera mounted on front.
    *   All defined in URDF/Xacro.

2.  **Environment:**
    *   A warehouse world in Gazebo with shelves and boxes.

3.  **Navigation:**
    *   Configure `nav2` stack.
    *   Robot must be able to autonomously navigate from Zone A to Zone B avoiding dynamic obstacles (walking people).

4.  **Deliverable:**
    *   A GitHub repository with the ROS 2 workspace.
    *   A video recording of the robot performing the navigation task.
