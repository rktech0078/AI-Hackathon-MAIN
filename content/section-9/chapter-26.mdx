# Chapter 26: Weekly Projects

Learning by doing is the only way to master robotics.

## Week 1: The Publisher
**Task:** Create a ROS 2 package `my_first_node` that publishes the current time every 1 second.
**Goal:** Understand Workspace, Package, Node, Publisher, Timer.

## Week 2: The Listener
**Task:** Create a subscriber node that listens to the time topic and calculates the time difference between messages.
**Goal:** Understand Subscriber, Callbacks, Latency.

## Week 3: Gazebo Spawn
**Task:** Create a URDF for a simple 2-wheeled robot and spawn it in an empty Gazebo world.
**Goal:** Understand URDF, Launch files, Gazebo Bridge.

## Week 4: Isaac Perception
**Task:** Use Isaac Sim to generate 100 images of a "Cube" with random colors and train a simple YOLO model to detect it.
**Goal:** Understand Synthetic Data, Computer Vision.
